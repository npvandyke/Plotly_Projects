Welcome to the Belly Button Biodiversity Dashboard! This interactive page is based off a dataset documenting the bacterial cultures found in the belly buttons of 152 anonymous volunteer test subjects. Begin by selecting a subject based on their ID number in the panel to the left below.

The panel will display the subject's corresponding demographic information, while the three charts below will populate with their other related data. The bar chart displays the top 10 bacterial cultures found in the subject's belly button, labeled by the bacteria's operational taxonomic unit (OTU). The name of each OTU ID number is displayed when you hover above the bar. The gauge chart displays the belly button washing frequency of the subject. The bubble chart displays all of the bacterial cultures found in the subject's belly button, with OTU ID indicated by color and the sample size of the bacterial culture indicated by the size of the bubble.

Have fun interacting with the visualizations, and for a closer look at the full sample set, click on the link above!
